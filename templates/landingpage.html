Features

Everything you need to manage tasks

TaskFlow provides all the tools you need to create, assign, and track tasks across your team.

Task Boards

Create multiple boards to organize different projects and workflows. Customize each board to fit your team's needs.

Team Collaboration

Invite team members to your boards, assign tasks, and collaborate in real-time with comments and notifications.

Task Management

Create, edit, and delete tasks. Mark tasks as complete and track progress with visual indicators.

Deadlines & Reminders

Set due dates for tasks and receive reminders to stay on track. View tasks in a calendar view.

Progress Tracking

Monitor project progress with visual dashboards and reports. Identify bottlenecks and optimize workflows.

Permissions & Security

Control who can view, edit, and delete tasks and boards. Keep your data secure with role-based permissions.
